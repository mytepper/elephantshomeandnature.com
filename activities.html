Elephant mud spa and bathing

Duration: 1 hour



Description: This program is the best option for those people who want to spoil a little bit the elephant before a quick ride to the river where they have the chance to play and bath with the animal. After meeting your elephant we will move to the mud spa where you will have the chance to give him a pleasant treatment. After spending some time playing and rolling in the mud it will be your job to give him a good massage using both mud and fruit. Both of you at this time will be happy and dirty and everyone will need a good bath in the river. With the mahout close to you all the time you will ride in the river and the elephant will start having fun moving quickly up and down in the water. Your big challenge will be to be able to stay on the elephant. If you cannot swim don’t worry; just let the staff and the mahout know that and the game will be a bit softer and always in the part of the river where the water is shallow enough for you and the current is not strong. After this it’s time to relax again: the elephant will sit down and will wait for you to wash and scrub them. You will see your animal pleased, happy and gentle. We guarantee you will want this moment to never end. Then it’s time for some pictures. Cute, funny, joyful or brave pictures! You can hug the elephant, he will lift you up with his trunk, you can kiss him and he will kiss you back. Amazing memories that will last forever. Before your program ends you’ll be able to ride the elephant back for the last goodbye!

Notes: Please note that you’ll be able to ride only bareback. We don’t use any basket chair. They’re too heavy and it’s all unnecessary extra weight for the elephant. Plus the bareback riding make you feel a lot closer to the animal and the experience is totally different and definitely better. During your riding you don’t have to worry about pictures. Our staff and/or your tour guide (if you have one) will take pictures for you with your own camera. After your program you can have a shower. We provide shampoo and soap. Just remember to bring your towel and clothes to change.

What to bring: waterproof sunblock, hat, sunglasses, mosquito spray, camera or mobile for pictures (if you have a waterproof camera bring it with you for amazing shots in the river), bathing suit or changing clothes, and towel.
